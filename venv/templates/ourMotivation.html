Our Motivation

Why A Flight Time Logger?


A C-130 Loadmaster poses near an open ramp during an oceanic flight

This application serves a purpose for individuals who need to track and generate reports for their aircraft flight time.

Pilots, loadmasters, flight engineers, and flight attendants are required to log the aircraft on which they fly, their airborne time, location data, and cargo data.

This data is collected by various private and public agencies and analyzed to increase flight safety, record personnel information, and prevent illegal cargo smuggling.

Most flight agencies, including those in the US Department of Defense, depend on web applications to track and share hours, and this is our take on an application that could be useful to those agencies.
